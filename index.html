DJI FlyCart 100

A Revolution in Aerial Logistics


DJI has officially introduced the FlyCart 100 (FC100), the new flagship heavy-lift industrial drone. Designed to operate across extreme terrains, the FC100 redefines what is possible in aerial cargo delivery.


Key Technical Advantages


  • Maximum Payload: Up to 100 kg (single battery) and 85 kg (dual battery mode).
  • LiDAR: High-precision 3D terrain mapping for obstacle detection.
  • Penta-Vision System: Five-directional visual sensors with 360° coverage.
  • Millimeter-Wave Radar: Reliable obstacle detection in all weather conditions.
  • Integrated Parachute: Emergency deployment reduces descent speed to 6–7 m/s.

Dual Payload System


  1. Flagship Winch System: 30-meter cable with electric hook enables mid-air payload lifting and lowering without landing.
  2. Cargo Container System: Secure, fixed compartment for standard cargo transportation.
